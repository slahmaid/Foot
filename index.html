The Origin of "304"

Born Lamine Yamal Nasraoui Ebana on July 13, 2007, his dazzling footwork was forged on the unforgiving concrete courts of Rocafonda, a working-class neighborhood in Mataró. After outclassing older kids and shining at his local club, CF La Torreta, the seven-year-old prodigy was snapped up by FC Barcelona's famed La Masia academy in 2014. Today, despite his rapid rise to global stardom, he ensures his humble beginnings are never forgotten—celebrating every goal by flashing "304" with his fingers to immortalize his hometown's 08304 postal code on the world stage.

Player Profile

Lamine Yamal is the archetypal modern winger, blending explosive pace with a rare, innate footballing intelligence. As his metrics suggest, his standout trait is his world-class dribbling; he possesses a low center of gravity and devastating 1v1 ability that allows him to effortlessly dismantle deep defensive blocks and create space out of nothing.

Beyond just raw speed, his exceptional passing and vision mean he operates not just as a touchline threat, but as a primary playmaker. Whether he is cutting inside to unleash a curled strike from his lethal left foot or threading line-breaking passes to overlapping teammates, Yamal dictates the tempo of the final third with a maturity and composure that entirely defies his age.

Global Brand & Partnerships

Adidas

Yamal signed a long-term endorsement partnership with Adidas in February 2024, valued at an estimated $2 million annually. As part of the deal, he wears the 'X Crazyfast' boots and became the first player to officially endorse Lionel Messi's signature 'Spark Gen10s' line. In August 2025, his role expanded as he became a global ambassador for Adidas Originals.

American Eagle

In January 2026, Yamal was announced as a global brand ambassador for American Eagle in a historic five-year deal. This partnership includes talent-led campaigns, such as the "Ready for the World" soccer campaign, and limited-edition product collaborations.

Visa

He holds an active endorsement agreement with Visa, which yields an annual value of $1.5 million.

Beats Electronics

Yamal secured a $1 million per year deal with Beats Electronics. He was prominently featured in their "Lamine Records" campaign.

Konami

He maintains a sponsorship agreement with the video game and entertainment company Konami, valued at $1 million annually.

OPPO

In January 2025, OPPO announced Yamal as their global brand ambassador to lead their "Make Your Moment" initiative. This partnership is valued at $500,000 per year.

Powerade

Yamal also has a sponsorship deal with Powerade, worth an estimated $500,000 annually.

DICK's Sporting Goods

In June 2026, Yamal was featured in a World Cup-focused collaborative campaign between Adidas and DICK's Sporting Goods titled "Where It All Kicks Off". He also fronted a SP5DER x Adidas campaign in March 2026 that bridged streetwear and football.
